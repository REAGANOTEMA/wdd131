Site Purpose

The site aims to provide a centralized learning hub for digital skills, including programming, design, freelancing, and entrepreneurship, tailored for young Ugandans. It will offer tutorials, registration for skill bootcamps, mentor access, and community engagement.

Scenarios

  • Where can I register for upcoming skill training bootcamps near Kampala?
  • What free resources are available to learn web development from scratch?

Color Scheme

  • Pink (#E91E63): Accent color for highlights, section headings border.
  • Red (#D32F2F): Buttons, hover states, and some text highlights.
  • Dark Blue (#1A237E): Header background, main headings, and footer text.
  • Yellow (#FBC02D): Background accents, wireframe container, subtle animations.

Typography

  • Merriweather: Used for headings to convey elegance and professionalism.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text for readability and modern style.

Wireframe

Mobile View

Logo at top center, hamburger menu on left, hero section with large CTA button, featured programs stacked vertically, footer with contact info and social links.

Desktop View

Horizontal nav bar with logo left, menu center, login/register right. Large hero banner with headline and button. Below, a three-column grid for programs, sidebar for testimonials, and footer with full contact and socials.
