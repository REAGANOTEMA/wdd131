SkillSprint Uganda – Strategic Website Planning

Site Name

SkillSprint Uganda A name that represents a national initiative to fast track practical digital skills development for young Ugandans.

Optional Domain: skillsprint.ug

Purpose

The website will serve as a digital hub for learning, mentorship, and career growth in Uganda. It will offer bootcamp registration, tutorials in tech and freelancing, and access to a support community of mentors and learners.

User Scenarios

  • “Where can I register for the next skill training bootcamp near Kampala?”
  • “Are there free beginner tutorials for web development or freelancing?”

Color Scheme

The chosen palette emphasizes energy, trust, and accessibility:

  • Dark Blue (#1A237E): Trustworthy and professional – used for headers and navigation.
  • Pink (#E91E63): Energetic accents for call-to-action and highlights.
  • Red (#D32F2F): Used sparingly for urgency, buttons, or warnings.
  • Yellow (#FBC02D): Bright, positive background highlights or badges.

Typography

  • Merriweather: Headlines and section titles gives the site a trustworthy and bold feel.
  • Open Sans: Paragraphs and UI text modern, clean, and readable.

Wireframes

Visual structure of the home page layout in both mobile and desktop formats:

Mobile Layout

Centered logo, hamburger menu, stacked hero with CTA, vertically listed programs, footer with contact & socials.

Desktop Layout

Top horizontal nav (logo-left, menu-center, auth-right), banner with headline and action button, content grid (3 columns), and full footer with details.
