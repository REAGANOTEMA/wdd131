About Uganda

Uganda is a beautiful country located in East Africa. Known as the "Pearl of Africa," it boasts stunning landscapes, wildlife, and warm, friendly people. From the source of the Nile in Jinja to the lush national parks, Uganda offers a rich cultural and natural heritage. English is the official language, and the nation is known for its resilience, talent, and innovation.
